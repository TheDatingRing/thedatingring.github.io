To the Dating Ring Community:

After an amazing five years of getting the chance to set up members, Dating Ring shut its doors on August 31, 2018.

We set out to change the landscape of dating for good­—to transform a traditionally frustrating experience into a positive one—and to bring more love into the world. And for five years, we were lucky enough to get the chance to do just that.

Best of luck in all of your dating adventures. If there's anything we've learned as matchmakers, it's to remember to always be as open as possible to meeting new people. Erase those filters, be kind, and get out there and have a good time!

xo,

Lauren, Emma, Shearly, and the Dating Ring team
